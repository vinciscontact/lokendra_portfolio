(01) — About

Light, motion and silence — shaped into stories. I work between the still frame and the moving image, chasing the moments most people scroll past.

I'm Lokendra, a self-driven videographer and photographer with a growing love for cinematography. From bold vertical reels to quiet, high-contrast stills, my work lives in the tension between dark and light.

I shoot it, I cut it, and increasingly I write the story underneath it. Every project is a chance to make a viewer pause for one second longer.
